About Me

I’m a Full Stack Developer with a background in Software Test Engineering across a breadth of programs and technologies in the medical device industry. I am driven by the complexity of a world that generates and consumes vast amounts of data as well as the tools we use to automate software. With that being said, web development fuels my passion for creativity through problem solving. I am fascinated by what programming can accomplish from anywhere in the world. For the past 6 months, I participated in an intensive coding Boot Camp dedicated to designing and building web applications at the University of Minnesota.

I gained skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, bootstrap, AngularJS, Firebase, Ionic Mobile Framework, Node JS, MySQL, mongoDB, Express, and handlebars JS. I’m ready to put my knowledge gained from real time project development into creating automated web applications.
